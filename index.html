Events

Arduino Workshop

Workshop was conducted for 2nd year students which included introduction to microcontroller basics taking Arduino as example and interfacing of various sensors. Based on participants interest different elaborated aspects of the respective subjects were discussed.

aMAZE

A technofun online game with 48 hours timeline. A maze to complete by solving technical questions with an interesting story line. Participants were required to think as the fictional heroes of their chosing and reach their destination within the stipulated time by solving various puzzels.

Workshop on SQL

This workshop was an introduction to sql. This included introduction to basic commands of sql on microsoft's sql server platform. The second part included a handson session on the same. Participants were given various tasks to perform for the better understanding.
