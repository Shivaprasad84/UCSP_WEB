Events

Arduino Workshop

Workshop was conducted for 2nd year students which included introduction to microcontrollers basics and interfacing various sensors. Based of participants intrest different elaborated aspects of the respective subjects were discussed.

aMAZE

A technofun online game with 48 hours timeline. A maze to complete by solving technical questions with an interesting story line. Participants were required to think as the fictional heroes of their chosing and reach their destination within the stipulated time.

Workshop on SQL

Workshop was an introduction to sql. This included introduction to basic commands of sql on microsoft's sql server platform. The second part included a handson session on the same. Participants were given various tasks to perform for the better understanding.

Our Motivation

Mission

See what everyone has seen, but think what nobody else has thought and bring a change in the society.

Vision

To build a research environment for inspiring minds and to embark intellectual circuit in the years to come.

Goals

To create awareness and keen intrest in today's trending technologies and to orient the students and faculties alike to undertake research in their selected areas of intrest.
